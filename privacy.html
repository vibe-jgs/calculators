Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

The Super Wealth Calculator is committed to ensuring your privacy and protecting your personal financial information. This Privacy Policy outlines our compliance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

2. 100% Client-Side Architecture

This application is engineered with a strict serverless, client-side architecture. This means:

  • All calculations are performed locally within your own web browser.
  • No financial data, personal information, or calculation results are ever transmitted to, collected by, or stored on our servers.
  • You are not required to create an account, log in, or provide any identifying information (such as your name or email address) to use this tool, ensuring complete anonymity in compliance with APP 2.

3. Data at Rest & Local Storage

To improve your user experience, the calculator uses your browser's localStorage to temporarily "remember" the numbers you input, so you don't have to retype them if you accidentally refresh the page.

  • Enterprise Encryption: Any financial data saved to your local storage is cryptographically encrypted using an ephemeral AES-GCM session key.
  • Auto-Shredding: Because the decryption key is stored only in your active browser session, your saved data is permanently crypto-shredded and rendered unreadable the moment you close your browser tab.

4. Clearing Your Data

You have full control over your local data. You can instantly wipe all saved configurations and encrypted data by clicking the "Clear My Data & Reset" button located in the footer of the calculator.

5. Analytics and Tracking

This website does not currently use cookies for tracking, nor do we employ third-party analytics scripts (such as Google Analytics) that monitor your behavior.

6. Changes to this Policy

If we introduce optional features that require data transmission in the future, we will update this policy and request your explicit consent before any data leaves your device.
